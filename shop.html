FLASHFORGE Adventurer 5M 3D Printer

Price: $379.00

Buy on Amazon

Product Overview:

Experience the future of 3D printing with the Adventurer 5M Series. The AD5M uses a Core XY structure, with a travel speed of 600mm/s and 20000mm/s² acceleration.

It offers rapid nozzle changes, a high-flow 32mm³/s nozzle for stable high-speed printing, dual-sided PEI platform for easy removal, and multiple platform options. It's a smart and efficient choice for creative projects.

The printer features dual-fan nozzles, vibration compensation, multiple nozzle sizes (0.25, 0.4, 0.6, 0.8mm), and a 280°C direct extruder, catering to a wide variety of materials.

Additionally, it supports a mobile app—Flash Maker—allowing for remote real-time monitoring of print progress, adjustment of parameters, and receiving message alerts.
